Features

Personalized E-cards Organized Collection Private Note & Tags Files Attachment Scan, Connect, Invite Chat

A cleaner, smarter way to maximize your opportunity.

  • Digitize your paper business card into a smart digital profile.
  • Tap to expand information allowing you to feature critical information no business card would ever be able to fit.
  • Quickly edit and optimize your profile and even include clickable links so your new contacts will be able to easily find out what they need to know.
  • Effortless template makes it easy to build a digital business card from scratch, customized to suit your needs.

Organize contacts in your own way

  • Customize contacts into “collections” based on when, where, and who introduced you so you never lose the context of an important meeting.
  • Create custom categories and groups to suit your organizational needs.
  • Manage by tags, and levels of importance.

Never forget where and who you met again!

  • Make Important notes about the contact you just met, leads, new products, and important conversations in real time within the app before so you can reference it later whenever you need to.
  • Too busy to take notes, or don’t like typing? Not a problem- snap a quick photo or screenshot and save to your notes.

Show off your sales catalogue, or portfolio directly from your profile.

  • Share files directly, and right away. PDF, pictures, videos, and more!
  • Attach files, photos, and videos OR direct your new contacts to specific information and make a more meaningful first impression.

Leverages your Influence with the latest in green technology.

  • BizNect uses an enhanced Optical Character Recognition Technology to let you easily scan business cards and store their contact information and manage these digital files on your smartphone. What if they don’t have BizNect account? No worries, it’s easy to invite people - and they will thank you for it later!
  • Easily connect with people nearby and exchange contact information in Wi-Fi zone or via bluetooth connection. Simply send an invitation to the person you want to connect with.

Instant chat with those you meet. Make things happen.

  • Straight Talk. Increase Influence. Real-time conversation means more opportunities. With Built-in chat, you can quickly follow up with the person you just connected with, manage, and respond to all of your chat conversations right in the app.
  • Share important images, files, and more, directly in the chat. Keep the conversation going smoothly without the need for ever leaving the app.
