A better way to network.

  • Digitize paper business cards into smart digital profiles.
  • Tap-to-expand design lets you display critical information a business card could never fit. See the story behind the labels.
  • Quickly optimize your profile with visual media and clickable links so prospects can see everything you want them to- quickly and easily.
  • Our Effortless-Templates make it easy to build digital business card profiles for any occasion.

Organize contacts in your own way

  • Customize contacts into “collections” based on when, where, and who introduced you so you never lose the context of an important meeting.
  • Create custom categories and groups to suit your organizational needs.
  • Manage contacts by tags, and levels of importance.

Keep track of the Details

  • Take notes about the prospect you just met to remind yourself of conversations, products, leads and other important information. Use as a reference later at your convenience.
  • Too busy to take notes, or don’t like typing? Not a problem- snap a quick photo or screenshot and save to your notes on any contact.

Show off your sales catalogue, or portfolio directly from your profile.

  • Share files directly, and right away. PDF, pictures, videos, and more!
  • Attach files, photos, and videos OR direct your new contacts to specific information and make a more meaningful first impression.

  • BizNect uses an enhanced Optical Character Recognition Technology to let you easily scan business cards and store their contact information and manage these digital files on your smartphone. Message prospects and Make things happen.
